Services

Our services range from Clinical, Surgical, Medical, Diagnostic, Pharmaceutical and Nursing Services.

Clinical Services

Specialist clinics at AAR hospital are located at the doctor’s plaza within the main hospital complex allowing for convenience to see patients and muilti disciplinary approach to care.

Diagnotics

The Diagnostics Services and Health Information Division provides Radiological, Laboratory, Farewell and Health Information services to support clinical excellence.

Medical

Medical-Surgical care at MedicalCare Hospital with dedicated staff offering evidence-based healthcare using innovative technology. Patient care shall is provided using an interdisciplinary approach involving the patient, nursing, medical staff, pharmacy, laboratory, pathology, respiratory therapy, spiritual care, nutritional services, rehabilitation, and radiology.

Pharmaceutical

We operate 24 hours providing quality, genuine and affordable drugs, and surgical items both for inpatient, outpatient, and external prescribed drugs for clients. The Pharmacy is well stocked with a wide range of quality generic and branded medicines.

Critical Care

MedicalCare Hospital accident and emergency unit is located on ground floor with its own dedicated entrance specializing in emergency medicine. It is a 24-hour urgent care unit that will handle all the emergency cases and a wide range of treatment services, diagnostic test, and minor surgical procedures.

Endoscopy Service

Endoscopy is the field of medicine that examines the inside of the body to diagnose, evaluate, and treat conditions of the oesophagus, stomach, and portions of the intestine. MedicalCare Hospital provides a one-stop approach for diagnostic and therapeutic gastrointestinal and bronchoscope procedures on an outpatient, in-house, and on emergency basis.

Cardiology

Cardiologists are doctors who have extra education and training in preventing, diagnosing and treating heart conditions.

Our cardiologist is well able to treat chest pain, high blood pressure and heart failure, as well as problems with your heart valves, blood vessels and other heart and vascular issues.

Neurology

A neurologist is a medical doctor who diagnoses, treats and manages disorders of the brain and nervous system.

The neurologist at MedicalCare is equipped to treat disorders that affect the brain, spinal cord, and nerves

Hepatologist

Hepatologists are medical doctors who diagnose, treat, and manage problems associated with your liver, gallbladder, bile ducts, and pancreas.

MedicalCare Hospital has the best Hepatologist doctors that have helped many in dealing with liver problems.
